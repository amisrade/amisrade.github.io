Women of the World Website Redesign

About the Project

Goal: Redesign the Women of the World Website to improve user functionality and overall website aesthetics and friendliness.

Type: Product Design, Concept Duration: 1 month, Nov 2022

Skills: UX/UI design, Interaction and Visual Design

Responsibility: I designed this Website to further my UX/UI skills

Challenge

Women of the World is an Non-For-Profit that looks to empower and help women that have been forcibly displaced in society from every corner of the world. The site has a very colorful design that looks to show it’s multi-cultural design. At first glance, it does make it hard to take in the information - so our mission is to maintain that warmth and attract the eyes around the page but all the while allow for the information to be easily absorbed.
