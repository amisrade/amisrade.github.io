“PIN POINT” Travel App

About the Project

Goal:Create a lowfi onboarding process, of a user-friendly travel app that helps travelers conveniently organize their travel plans and schedules

Type:Product Design, ConceptDuration: 1 month, September 2022

Skills: UX/UI design, Interaction and Visual Design

Responsibility: I designed this application to further my UX/UI skills and understanding

Challenge

Travelers are often left stressed out and disorganized when planning a trip, So i developed an app that helps travelers make the most out of their trips by conveniently scheduling plans together and finding fun travel activities

I created Dakota to be the User Persona, Since disorganized and busy people with an appetite to travel, like him, lack time management skills.

Making this User persona helped narrowing it down to what audience we need to tailor the PINPOINT app too.

The Story board process involving Dakota, really helped creating a unique senerio, that would likely happen to most people.

When I created the user flow for PinPoint, I got a better sense of how the on boarding process would look like.

With the user flow completed, I drew up a LoFi mock up of the on boarding process of the app.

Im a visual person so seeing this drawn up really helped with the design making of the app.

Using Figma, I made a low fidelity prototype of the onboarding process. Its a very simple design but It takes care of bussiness.

I faced a learning curve since Figma was new to me at the time but It was worth the learning experience to get through with it

Final Thoughts

The Pinpoint project was a good early project to help me get my feet wet into this field. I learned a lot through it. On the analytical side of this project, I was able be more immersed in the UX side of things which definitely was a plus.
